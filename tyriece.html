Tyriece and Marlon's Wedding

Visual Identity | Art Direction | Packaging Design

Tools: Adobe Photoshop & InDesign

Project Overview

In 2016, Tyriece and Marlon asked me to create storybook-esque invitations for their wedding in April. They wanted to include a personable photo and send out greetings in a playful vibe that showcased their love and personalities.

During their engagement photoshoot at a local park, they took a photo in a peek-a-boo manner with a park utility column. To romantiscize the photo. I completed an edit that replaced the background with more shrubs and the column with a heart etched tree.
