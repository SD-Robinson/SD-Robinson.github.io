BlossomTime

UX Design | Product Strategy | Research | Visual Identity | Art Direction

Tools: Figma | Adobe Photoshop | Illustrator | Google Jamboard

Duration: February 2023- June 2023

Project Overview

BlossomTime is a trendy florist company that provides flower selections to customers with busy schedules. They wanted to develop a mobile app to support their ordering, scheduling, and delivery services. The goal of the app was to provide a dynamic floral gifting experience to customers who need assistance in creating special moments for all occasions while on the go and in tight timeframes.
